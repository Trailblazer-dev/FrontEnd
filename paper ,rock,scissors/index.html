About Rock-Paper-Scissors

Welcome to our Rock-Paper-Scissors game! This classic hand game is a simple yet entertaining way to make decisions or just have fun.

The game is believed to have originated in China over 2,000 years ago and has since become a popular pastime around the world.

In Rock-Paper-Scissors, two players face off and simultaneously form one of three shapes with their hand:

  • Rock: Represented by a clenched fist.
  • Paper: Represented by an open hand
  • Scissors: Represented by a fist with the index and middle fingers extended, forming a V.
The rules are straightforward:
  • Rock crushes Scissors
  • Scissors cut Paper
  • Paper covers Rock

Our online version of Rock-Paper-Scissors brings this timeless game to your fingertips, allowing you to challenge the computer. Enjoy the game, test your strategy, and may the best player win!

How to Play Rock-Paper-Scissors

Welcome to Rock-Paper-Scissors! If you're new to the game, here's a quick guide to help you get started:

  1. The Basics:

    • Rock-Paper-Scissors is played between two players. Each player chooses one of the three shapes: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
    • Players reveal their chosen shapes simultaneously.

  2. The Rules:

    • Rock crushes Scissors.
    • Scissors cut Paper.
    • Paper covers Rock.
    • If both players choose the same shape, the round is a tie, and you play again.

  3. Playing the Game:

    • Step 1: Choose your shape (Rock, Paper, or Scissors).
    • Step 2: Reveal your shape when prompted.
    • Step 3: The game will compare the shapes and determine the winner based on the rules.
    • Step 4: The winner is announced, or if it's a tie, you play another round.
